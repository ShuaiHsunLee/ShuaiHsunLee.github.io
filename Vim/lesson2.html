Normal Mode

It's really worth spending time on Normal Mode!!!

Movement 1

Times + Movement

  • numbers: 1,2,3,4,...
  • move: h,j,k,l
  • word(forward): w, e
  • word(backward): b, ge
  • jump into nth line: gg

Movement 2

same line: 0, $, ^, g_

paragraph: {, }, (, )

first line: gg

last line: G

Current Page: H, M, L

Action 1

Verb + Times + Noun

  • yank: y
  • cut/delete: d
  • change: c
  • number: 1,2,3,...
  • once: a
  • word: w
  • paragraph: p
  • movement: h, j, k, l, 0, ^, $, g_
  • inside: i
  • brace: ', ", <, (, [, {
  • tag for html: t

Action 2

yank a line: yy

cut/delete a line: dd

change a line: cc

------------------------------------------------------

paste content from: yank, cut/delete/change

paste: p, P, ]p, ]P
